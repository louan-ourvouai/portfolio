About

I’m Louan Ourvouai, a Unity gameplay engineer and ENSIIE (Paris–Évry) graduate, an engineering school specialized in computer science and mathematics, with a Graduate Engineer degree, JIN track (Video Games and Digital Interactions). I’ve built Unity projects across mobile, VR, Windows, and macOS, covering genres such as RTS, FPS/TPS, idle, 2D platformers, and serious games. I previously led the development of a mobile RTS, and I’m currently leading a pet-care mobile game project. Beyond programming, I enjoy contributing across the whole creation pipeline like visuals, sound, narrative, and game design to help craft cohesive player experiences.
